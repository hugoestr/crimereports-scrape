Crime Report: June 25, 2012

June 25, 2012
REPORTS
 
PROSTITUTION, 06/21/12, 3000 block of S. Columbia Pike. Between 7 and 10 pm on June 21, subjects were observed by police operating a prostitution business from several hotel rooms. The subjects had been advertising their services on an onlince solicitation site. Carmen Ponce, 30, of Manassas, VA, was arrested and charged with prostitution. Sarah Jones, 20, of no fixed address, was arrested and charged with prostitution and keeping a bawdy place. Zhiwei Jiao, 48, of no fixed address, was arrested and charged with prostitution  keeping a bawdy place. Leslie Celenia Torres Elvir, 24, of Oxon Hill, MD, was arrested and charged with receiving money from earnings of prostitution and using a vehicle to promote prostitution. Nurgazy Ibraimov, 19, of Arlington, VA, was charged with solicitation of a prostitute. Gilbert Bowen, 70, of Bethesda, MD, was charged with solicitation of a prostitute. Julian Pedro Vicente Paxtor, 38, of Falls Church, VA, was charged with solicitation of a prostitute. Michael Surber, unknown age, of unknown address, was charged with solicitation of a prostitute. Jason Gooljar, 33, of Arlington, VA, was charged with solicitation of a prostitute.
 
SEXUAL BATTERY, 06/24/12, 3000 block of S. Columbia Pike. Between 11:30 am and 12:30 pm on June 23, a victim was sexually assaulted while getting a massage at a salon. The subject is described as a Hispanic male, in his late 20s to early 30s, approximately 6’4” and 200 lbs. He had black curly hair in a pony tail and stated his name was Juan.
 
ASSAULT BY MOB, 06/24/12, 1300 block of N. Fillmore Street. On June 24 at 1:19 am, three subjects assaulted three victims after a verbal argument. The three victims were transported to a local hospital with minor injuries. Kenneth Bailey Jr., 21, of Arlington, VA, Yohana Yemani Abed, 21, of Arlington, VA and Cochise Dajon Morris, 21, of Alexandria, VA, were arrested and charged with assault by mob. They were held without bond.
 
BURGLARY, 06/22/12, 3500 block of S. 16th Street. Between 7:15 am and 5:30 pm on June 21, an unknown subject(s) entered a residence through a bathroom window and stole various items of jewelry. There is no suspect description.
